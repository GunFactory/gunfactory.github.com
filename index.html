Power, Greed, Guilt

A war between two nations is imminent. There is opportunity to make profit out of the incoming war. What path are going to take?

War Agent is a strategy resource management game that puts the player in the place of a war profiteer. The game explores the world of war profiteering and how greed can lead to unforeseen consequences on a global scale.
