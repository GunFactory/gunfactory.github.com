    • The Team

    Drift On Studio is composed of four members with the common interest of making games. War Agent is our first mobile game in development. The team made its debut on June 2013 with the help of Critical Hit, a summer program that brings together artists, programmers and developers to produce games in 10 weeks with the help of mentors from the academia and game industry.







    Mehrdad Dehdashti

    Mehrdad Dehdashti was born in Tehran, Iran. He lived there for 18 years untill he came to Canada. He graduated from McGill university with a degree of computer science in 2013. He is interested in both technical and artistic aspects of games development. He has created games since his childhood and wants to continue making games as he believes there is so much more to be explored in games. You can find his website here

    Thuy Linh Tran

    Thuy Linh Tran was born and raised in Montreal, Canada. Graduated in Graphic Design at Vieux Montreal Cegep in 2010, she studied for a year and a half in Computation Arts at Concordia University where she finally decided to turn her passion for 3D and games into a career. She is currently studying in 3D Animation & Digital Design at NAD Centre to specialize in concept art and character design. She has always loved the visual aspect in gaming. The artistic direction that immerses the player into the game's world have inspired her to someday create one of her own with digital tools. You can find her portfolio here
