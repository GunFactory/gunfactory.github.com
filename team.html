    • The Team

    Bazinu Inc is composed of four members with the common interest of making games. War Agent is our first mobile game in development. The team made its debut on June 2013 with the help of Critical Hit, a summer program that brings together artists, programmers and developers to produce games in 10 weeks with the help of mentors from the academia and game industry.



    Mehrdad Dehdashti graduated from McGill university with a degree of computer science in 2013. He is interested in both technical and artistic aspects of games development. He has created games since his childhood and wants to continue making games as he believes there is so much more to be explored in games. You can find his website here


    Elise Hamzaoglu graduated in Fine Arts at Vieux Montreal Cegep in 2009. She then decided to study in the 3D Animation & Digital Design program from Nad Centre. With that knowledge, she wishes to eventually be able to model and design games. You can find her portfolio here


    Alexandre Kishka is a computer science student at McGill university. His first interest was life sciences, but then decided to switch to computer science. This is when he decided to turn his passion for games into his career.


    Thuy Linh Tran graduated in Graphic Design at Vieux Montreal Cegep in 2010. She is currently studying in 3D Animation & Digital Design at NAD Centre to specialize in concept art and character design. The artistic direction that immerses the player into the game's world have inspired her to someday create one of her own with digital tools. You can find her portfolio here
